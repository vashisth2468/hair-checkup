# 💇 Hair Checkup

### AI-Powered Shampoo Recommendation System
> Find your perfect shampoo in 2 minutes.
> Based on your hair type, scalp condition, and symptoms — not hype, not price, not design.

---

## 🌐 Live Demo
👉 [**Try Hair Checkup Live**](https://hair-checkup-we4t.vercel.app/)

---

## 📌 The Problem
Every day millions of people buy the wrong shampoo based on:
* **Brand hype** and influencer marketing.
* **Packaging design** and fragrance.
* **Price traps** or random discounts.
* **Switching fatigue** — changing products every month without results.

Nobody asks the most important question: **"Is this right for MY hair?"**
Most companies hide complex ingredient lists in tiny print because informed users would choose differently.

---

## 💡 The Solution
**Hair Checkup** guides users through a 5-step science-backed diagnostic funnel. It strips away the marketing fluff to recommend products based on real compatibility between scalp chemistry and ingredient profiles.

---

## 📱 How It Works
1. **Step 1** → Select your hair type (Visual Stages)
2. **Step 2** → Tell us your scalp condition
3. **Step 3** → Select your symptoms (Dandruff, Hair Fall, etc.)
4. **Step 4** → Share your lifestyle (Active, Indoor, Swimmer)
5. **Step 5** → Set your budget (Budget, Mid-range, Premium)
6. **Result** → Get your **Top 3 Perfect Shampoos** with an ingredient breakdown.

## ✨ Features
* ✅ **Zero Login Required:** Immediate value for the user.
* ✅ **Mobile-First Design:** Optimized for the Indian smartphone user.
* ✅ **Real Indian Brands:** Includes Clinic Plus, Dove, Indulekha, Mamaearth, etc.
* ✅ **Ingredient Transparency:** Explains *why* an ingredient works in plain English.
* ✅ **Budget Logic:** Recommendations that respect your wallet.
* ✅ **Seasonal Tips:** Adapts advice for Indian Summers and Winters.
